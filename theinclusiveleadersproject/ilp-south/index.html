South Quadrant – Aligning

Theme: Ethics, Boundaries, Coherence, Continuity

Module 3.1 – Impact Assessment: Measure What Matters

Assess outcomes honestly and equitably.

Acquired Skills:
  • Performance analysis: Comparing outcomes to intended goals.
  • Indicator selection: Choosing metrics that matter.
  • Equity-based evaluation: Ensuring all voices are represented.

Takeaway: Leads a team reflection using 3 metrics and proposes one data-informed shift.

Module 3.2 – Change Catalysis: Spark What’s Stuck

Unblock stuck energy and catalyze movement.

Acquired Skills:
  • Change diagnostics: Identifying low-momentum areas.
  • Leverage point identification: Locating high-impact shifts.
  • Action prototyping: Testing small changes quickly.

Takeaway: Designs and leads a mini-intervention targeting one area of friction.

Module 3.3 – Stakeholder Engagement: Deepen the Invitation

Go beyond communication—build relationships.

Acquired Skills:
  • Stakeholder mapping: Understanding who holds value and needs voice.
  • Trust-building: Creating conditions for genuine involvement.
  • Co-creation facilitation: Inviting others into ownership.

Takeaway: Facilitates a stakeholder session with two new inclusion strategies.

Module 3.4 – Resource Mobilization: Power the Work Together

Coordinate time, people, and materials for momentum.

Acquired Skills:
  • Mobilization planning: Creating action-oriented logistics.
  • Role alignment: Assigning work clearly and fairly.
  • Operational coordination: Keeping things moving smoothly.

Takeaway: Launches a mini-project with a mobilization matrix and assigned roles.

Module 3.5 – Scalability Engineering: Expand with Integrity

Design for growth that doesn’t dilute your values.

Acquired Skills:
  • Scalability analysis: Understanding what expands well.
  • Replication design: Building models that travel.
  • Ethical expansion planning: Keeping integrity while scaling.

Takeaway: Outlines a scaling plan for a practice or pilot, naming what must stay the same.

Module 3.6 – Resilience Modeling: Build to Withstand

Prepare for disruption by building strength into your structure.

Acquired Skills:
  • Resilience factor mapping: Naming what strengthens your team.
  • Contingency design: Planning for breakdowns.
  • Stress-test readiness: Testing your structures in simulation.

Takeaway: Facilitates a team contingency scenario and stress-test walk-through.

Module 3.7 – Sustainability Integration: Embed What Endures

Make what works repeatable and self-renewing.

Acquired Skills:
  • Practice embedding: Turning wins into habits.
  • System continuity design: Planning to sustain.
  • Institutionalization planning: Designing for long-term resilience.

Takeaway: Develops a practice sustainability plan and updates implementation documents.

Module 3.8 – Legacy Formation: Pass It Forward

Ensure your impact lasts beyond your presence.

Acquired Skills:
  • Succession planning: Preparing others to carry the work.
  • Legacy documentation: Capturing what matters most.
  • Values transmission: Teaching through action and story.

Takeaway: Crafts a leadership succession map and legacy-sharing experience.
