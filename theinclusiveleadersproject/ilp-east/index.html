East Quadrant – Tracking

Theme: Perception, Systems, Patterns, Adjustment

Module 2.1 – System Mapping: See the Bigger Picture

Visualize the structures and actors shaping your context.

Acquired Skills:
  • System visualization: Creating visual representations of systems.
  • Stakeholder positioning: Identifying and locating key actors.
  • Network influence analysis: Understanding relational power and reach.

Takeaway: Facilitates a live stakeholder map and identifies 2 leverage nodes.

Module 2.2 – Process Flow: Trace the Movement

Trace how work happens and where it stalls.

Acquired Skills:
  • Workflow mapping: Diagramming sequences of operations.
  • Process diagnostics: Locating inefficiencies or confusion.
  • System improvement planning: Designing targeted refinements.

Takeaway: Guides team in mapping a workflow and proposing 1 process improvement.

Module 2.3 – Resource Alignment: Recalibrate Your Assets

Ensure that energy, time, and tools match strategy.

Acquired Skills:
  • Resource auditing: Reviewing resource distribution.
  • Strategic realignment: Repositioning assets for maximum effect.
  • Capacity planning: Estimating what's needed vs. available.

Takeaway: Conducts a team audit and produces a reallocation plan for key resources.

Module 2.4 – Feedback Loops: Tune Your Awareness

Create rhythms for responsive, inclusive feedback.

Acquired Skills:
  • Feedback loop design: Building pathways for response and reflection.
  • Data-informed iteration: Adjusting based on real inputs.
  • Learning system creation: Embedding reflection into structure.

Takeaway: Establishes a feedback loop system with review checkpoints.

Module 2.5 – Emergent Patterns: Make the Invisible Visible

Watch what forms over time. Learn to read trends before they speak too loudly.

Acquired Skills:
  • Pattern recognition: Spotting recurring structures or behaviors.
  • System sensing: Reading subtle organizational signals.
  • Reflective trend analysis: Interpreting what trends mean.

Takeaway: Facilitates team identification of 3 recurring patterns and their implications.

Module 2.6 – Adaptation Pathways: Stay Responsive

Plan for multiple futures, not just one.

Acquired Skills:
  • Scenario planning: Building strategy around several possible paths.
  • Responsive strategy: Pivoting based on real conditions.
  • Future-readiness modeling: Assessing preparedness across conditions.

Takeaway: Leads a team through scenario design and response modeling.

Module 2.7 – Strategic Planning: Chart the Course

Use what you've seen to plot an intelligent path forward.

Acquired Skills:
  • Strategic design: Building coherent and aligned long-term plans.
  • Milestone sequencing: Breaking vision into achievable stages.
  • Adaptive planning: Retaining flexibility within structure.

Takeaway: Produces a visual roadmap of initiatives and milestones.

Module 2.8 – Operational Revitalization: Realign the Day-to-Day

Make your routines match your strategy.

Acquired Skills:
  • Operational auditing: Reviewing current practices.
  • Realignment implementation: Adjusting day-to-day to support direction.
  • Performance optimization: Improving effectiveness without burnout.

Takeaway: Updates team operational calendar and embeds new rhythms.
