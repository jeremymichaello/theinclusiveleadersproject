Prepare for the Journey: A Flipped Approach

This curriculum uses a flipped learning model. The modules you are about to explore are designed as activities to be completed with your school or leadership teams prior to our training sessions.

This approach honors the collective wisdom you already hold. We suggest dedicating time during your Professional Learning Community (PLC) or leadership team meetings to engage with these materials.

The goal is not just preparation, but to begin the essential work of team building, fostering psychological safety, and creating a foundation of trust before we even begin.
